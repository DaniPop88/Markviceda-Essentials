About Us

Our Story
Markviceda Essentials Store was founded with a simple mission — to bring everyday goods closer to Filipino families, without compromising on quality or price. Proudly rooted in Quezon City, we’ve grown to become a trusted name in our barangay since opening our doors in 2021.

We believe in "serbisyong may malasakit", and that’s why we treat every customer like a neighbor. Whether you're buying just a sachet of shampoo or stocking up for the week, you’ll always be welcomed with a smile.
